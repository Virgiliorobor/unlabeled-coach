Intake · Vol. I
You're already doing the work.
You just haven't claimed it yet.

A structured practice of self-reflection that ends in action — for solo builders doing work that requires becoming someone slightly different in the process.

There's a difference between processing and moving. Most reflection stops at processing. The practice here ends with a commitment — something specific, something you can be held to. The insight only counts if it changes what you do in the next 48 hours.

The Principles
Four things that don't change.

Regardless of where you are. Regardless of which goal you're working on.

The resistance is the work.
Your avoidance isn't a character flaw. It's a pattern with a name. Once it's named, you can work with it instead of around it.
Reflection without action is just processing.
Every session ends with a commitment. Not a plan. Not a goal. A specific, actionable thing you will do before we talk again.
The coach adapts. The action doesn't.
It reads your context — your background, your goals, your pattern. The tone adapts. But the ending never changes: a specific, actionable commitment. No exceptions.
Completion is worth claiming.
When you do the thing — stopping to say I completed this is part of the practice. That moment closes the circle. Celebration here isn't a party. It's recognition.

The Session
Phases

Each goal moves through six phases independently. The coach meets you wherever the goal is.

Hover each phase to reveal what happens inside it.

Intake

02.
Reflection

03.
Clarity
The goal in one sentence. Precise enough to be wrong. That's the point.
TARGET LOCKED

Resistance
Your pattern, in context. Named in this goal specifically. Not in general.

Commitment
